Hi, Everyone! My wife and I met in Guild Wars 2 seven years ago and stream together on twitch.

On September 18th, 2021, led by Roy, a large number of Guild Wars 2 streamers participated in the juniSTRONG event in support of Juni, who was (and is) going through breast cancer surgery and ongoing treatments. Through the generosity of the most incredible gaming community in the world, the event raised more for us than we could ever have imagined and we wanted to express our gratitude to everyone involved!

This game, its creators, and its playerbase have changed our lives forever in so many ways. We're so grateful for your support and it's made such a huge difference for us. Thank you, everyone, so much. Words will never be enough.

Embedded above is a video Juni and I put out wanting to thank everyone personally, and listed below are everyone who participated as a streamer in the event, as well as those who donated to the GuFundMe. We're so grateful to you all!

We're also very thankful to Arenanet for highlighting the reddit post, retweeting the event on twitter and for fostering the amazing community Tyria engenders.

I also want to take a moment to thank everyone from the MTV Discord who have been keeping things going while we've been away. Our Moderators, Organizers and community members who volunteered their time to host events, help others, or even just to welcome newcomers, you've all been invaluable. A special thank you specifically to our friend Virus who invested so much.
